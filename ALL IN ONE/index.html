Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS)

PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Srikakulam was established to provide quality education to the children of transferable Central Government employees, including Defense and Para-Military personnel.

Academic Divisions

CBSE Curricula & Schooling Segments

Foundational Learning & Joyful Pedagogies

Our primary curriculum emphasizes child-centered, activity-based schooling. We focus on Hindi, English, mathematics, and environmental studies, coupled with physical education and creative drawing models.

  • Common Minimum Programme (CMP) activity rooms
  • E-learning smartboards in class lobbies
  • Toy-pedagogy and storytelling workshops
  • Regular sports rallies and cultural displays

Rigor, Languages, and Scientific Temperament

For middle and secondary levels, KVS mandates rigorous academic frameworks. Subjects include Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Social Sciences, Hindi, Sanskrit, and English, paired with AI & ICT labs.

  • Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs
  • AI, Coding, and Informatics Computer centers
  • Scouts & Guides patrol lines and adventure camps
  • National Level Social Science Exhibitions

Specialization, JEE/NEET Support, and Careers

Senior Secondary students specialize in Science (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology, Computer Science) or Commerce (Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, IP) options, alongside targeted career coaching classes.

  • Advanced science curriculum preparing for IIT-JEE/NEET
  • Informatics Practices (IP) & Python coding modules
  • Youth Parliament (YPs) and speech forums
  • Specialist career guidance and CBSE counseling
